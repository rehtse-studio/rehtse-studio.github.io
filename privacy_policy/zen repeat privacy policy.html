Zen Repeat Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 10, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines the data collection and usage practices of Rehtse Studio in relation to its mobile game, Zen Repeat.

No Data Collection

Rehtse Studio respects your privacy and does not collect any personal or non-personal information from users of Zen Repeat. This includes but is not limited to:

  • No user identification or device information is collected.
  • No gameplay data, statistics, or preferences are tracked.
  • No advertising or analytics services are integrated into the game.

Zen Repeat is designed to provide a purely offline and private gaming experience.

Third-Party Services

Zen Repeat does not utilize any third-party services or SDKs that might collect user data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Rehtse Studio reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at rehtse.studio@protonmail.com

By using Zen Repeat, you acknowledge and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
